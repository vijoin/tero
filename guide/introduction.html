Introduction

Tero is an open-source platform to create, share, and use AI agents that accelerate and improve software quality practices across your team. It is collaborative by design, secure by default, and extensible so you can adapt it to your workflows and tools.

Who is this for?

  • QA engineers, SDETs, and developers who want reusable AI agents that empowers them.
  • Team leads looking to adopt AI with security, governance, and cost controls.
  • Teams that need agents to run where they work (IDE, browser, CI) and fit existing workflows.

Why Tero?

  • Reusable and collaborative: Share agents and their knowledge across teams.
  • Secure by design: Use your preferred model/provider and deployment setup.
  • Integrated: Bring agents into your IDE and browser for everyday tasks.
  • Governed and measurable: Define agent tests, review and control usage, and track impact.
  • Extensible: Add tools and connect external capabilities as your needs grow.
